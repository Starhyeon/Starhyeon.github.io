Seasonal Experience

Once each season, our dining room shifts around a single idea—fire, snow, harvest, or sea.

Winter Tides Evening

For one weekend each winter, Sushi House dims the lights and leans fully into the cold. A set menu of warm broths, charcoal-kissed nigiri, and deeply comforting rice dishes is paired with aged sake and smoky highballs.

Limited seating is available at the counter and in the back tatami room. Reservations open one month in advance, and walk-ins are held for a few bar seats each night.

Interested in a private event or buy-out? Speak with our team at the end of your meal or email events@sushihouse.example.
